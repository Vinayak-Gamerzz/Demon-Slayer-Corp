Demon Slayer Corps

Hashiras

The Hashira are the nine elite warriors of the Demon Slayer Coprs. each representing a unique Breathing Style and possessing extraordinary strength. They are humanity's strongest defense against the Upper Rank demons and Muzan Kibutsuji himself.

Corps Ranks

Every Demon Slayer begins at the lowest rank and climbs through dedication, experience, and strength. Only the most exceptional warriors earn the title of Hashira, the highest rank within the Demon Slayer Corps.
