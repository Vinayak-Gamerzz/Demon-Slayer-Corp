The Beast of the Mountains

About

Inosuke is one of the main protagonists of demon slayer and a fearless member of the corps, Raised in the mountains by wild polar boars after losing his mother as a baby, he developved extraordinary instincts and survival skills

His aggressive Personality, unmatched confidence and unpredicatble fighting style make him one of the Corps, most unique swordsmen, Despite his rough behaviour, he gradually learns the value of friendship, teamwork, and compassion through his adventures with Tanjiro and Zenitsu

    • Appearance

    He is easily recognized by the boar's head mask he always wears, a keepsake from the wild boar the raised him. Under the mask, he has an unexpectedly youthful face with long black hair tipped in blue and bright green eyes, He wears the Standard Demon Slayer uniform but fights shirtless, showing his muscular physique

    Personality

    Inosuke is loud, fearless, competitive and incredibly energetic. He constantly challenges others to prove his strength and loves rushing headfirst into the battle!!

    Although he appears arrogant and wild, he sloly learns kindness teamwork and empathy from Tanjiro, Zenitsu and the people he meets during his journey

    Fight Style

    Inosuke created Beast Breathing, a unique breathing style inspired by animals and his life in the mountains. His fighting style is fast, unpredicatble and relies on instinct rather than formal techniques

    He fights using two serrated Nichirin Swords, allowing him to attack from unusual angles with incredible speed
