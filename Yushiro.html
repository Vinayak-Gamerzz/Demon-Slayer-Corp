Yushiro

Demon Artist

"Lady Tamayo will always come before anything else"

About

He is the only demon personally created by Tamayo and her most loyal companion. Unlike ordinary demons he never harms innocent people and spends his life protecting Tamayo while helping her research a way to defeat Muzan.

Although Yushiro often appears rude and short tempered he has a king heart beneath his cold attitude. His unwavering loyalty and unique Blood Demon Art make him one of the most valuable allies of the Demon Slayer Corps During the Final Battle

    • Appearance

    He has pale skin and short dark hair with green Highlights. He usually wears a light colored kimono and has a calm expression that quickly changes whenever someone speaks badly about Tamayo. His appearance is simple but memorable

    Personality

    Yushiro is serious stubborn and fiercely protective of Tamayo, He dislikes being around strangers and often acts cold toward others especially Tanjiro. Despite this he cares deeply about innocent people and willingly risks his life to help defeat Muzan

    He admiration for Tamayo is so great that he places her above anything else

    Fighting Style

    He prefers supporting his allies rather than fighting head on. He uses his Blood Demon Art to hide people create illusions and confuse enemies while carefully choosing the right moment to attack
