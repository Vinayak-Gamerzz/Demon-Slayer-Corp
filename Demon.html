About

Demons are the primary antagoinst in Demon Slayer. Once ordinary humans, they were transformed into immortal creatures after receiving the blood of Muzan Kibutsuji, the first and strongest demon

Possessing supernatural strength, rapid regeneration, and unique Blood Demon Arts, demons prey on humans to increase their power. Among them, the Twelve Kizuki stands as Muzan's most powerful followers, each possessing extraordinary abilities that challenge even the strongest Demon Slayers
