About

Giyu is one of the most respected and skilled members of the Demon Slayer Corps, serving as the Water Hashira. Calm, reserved, and heghly disciplined, he was the first Hashira introduced in the series and the first to recognize Tanjiro Kamado's determination to protect his demon sister, Nezuko. His decision to spare them changed the course of the entire story.

With years of combat experience and exceptional mastery of Water Breathing, Giyu has defeated countless demons and played a crucial role in the battle against Muzan Kibutsuji.

    • Appearance

    Giyu has long black hair tied into a low ponytail and striking dark blue eyes. He wears the standard Demon Slayer Corps Uniform covered by a unique half and half haori. One side is a solid dark red, while the other features a green, yellow, and orange geometric pattern representing the memories of her late sister Tsutako and his close friend Sabito.

    Personality

    Giyu is quiet, calm, and deeply introvert He rarely expresses his emotions, causing others to misunderstand him as cold or arrogant. In reality, he is compassionate and carries immense guilt over the deaths of people he cared about.

    Despite doubting his own worth as a Hashira, he always places the safety of others before himself. His unwavering sense of justice and compassion are evident when he protects Nezuko and supports Tanjiro despite opposition from the Corps.

    Fighting Style

    Giyu is one of the greatest users of Water Breathing, known for his precise movements, incredible speed, and defensie swordsmanship. His fighting style emphasizes fluidity, adaptability, and perfect comtrol.

    He also created his own original techniques

    Dead Calm (Eleventh Form)

    This technique allows him to nullify incoming attacks with near perfect precision, making it one of the strongest defensive techniques in the series.
