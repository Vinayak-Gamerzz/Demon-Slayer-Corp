Kanao Tsuyuri

The Flower Breathing Swordswoman

"From now on, follow your heart instead of leaving everything to chance"

About

Kanao Tsuyuri is skilled Demon Slayer and the adopted younger sister of Shinobu Kocho and Kanae Kocho, After a difficult childhood, she was rescued by the kocho sisters, who gave her a new life and trained her as a Demon Slayer.

Quiet, disciplined and exceptionally talented, kanao Initially struggles to express her emotions and often relies on a coin flip to make decisions as her journey progresses, she learns to trust her own heart, becoming one of the corps most powerful swordswomen.

    • Appearance

    Kanao has long black hair tied into a side ponytail with a butterfly shaped hairpin inherited from kanae kocho. She wears the standard Demon Slayer Corps uniform beneath a white cape and has soft purple eyes that reflect her calm nature

    Her graceful appearance perfectly complements her elegant swordsmanship

    Personality

    Kanao is calm, quiet, and highly disciplined. Due to the trauma of her childhood, she once struggled to express emotions and relied on flipping a coin to make decisions

    Over time, with encouragement from Tanjiro and the Kocho sisters, she gradually learns to trust her own feelings and becomes more open and compassionate

    Fight Style

    Kanao is a master of Flower Breathing, a graceful fighting style derived from Water Breathing, Her techniques emphasize speed, precision, and fluid movements, allowing her to overwhelm opponents with elegant yet deadly attacks

    Her exceptional eyesight enables her to read enemy movements with incredible accuracy
