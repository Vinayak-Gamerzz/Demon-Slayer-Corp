Entry Point Demon Slayer

Demon Slayer Corps

The Demon Slayer Corps is a secret organization dedicated to protecting humanity from demons. Its members undergo intense training, master powerful Breathing Styles, and wield Nichirin Blades to eliminate demons. At the top of the Corps stand the legendary Hashira the strongest swordsmen of their generation.

Demons

Demons are powerful creatures created by Muzan Kibutsuji. Possessing supernatural abilities, regeneration, and Blood Demon Arts, they are the greatest threat to humanity. Among them, the Upper Rank demons stand as the most terrifying enemies ever faced by the Demon Slayer Corps.

Breathing Styles

Breathing Styles are specialized swordsmanship techniques that greatly enhance a swordsman's physical abilities. From the graceful Water Breathing to the legendary Sun Breathing, each style offers its own unique forms, strengths, and legacy.

Nichirin Swords

Nichirin Blades are the signature weapons of Demon Slayers. Forged from Scarlet Crimson Iron Sand and Scarlet Ore, these special swords absorb sunlight the greatest weakness of demons. Their unique colors often reflect the wielder's fighting style and potential.
