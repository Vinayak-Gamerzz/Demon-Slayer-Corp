About

Kyogai is a former member of the Twelve Kizuki who appears during the Tsuzumi Mansion Arc. Even after losing his rank he continues searching for recognition and believes that becoming stronger is the only way to prove his worth

Before becoming a demon kyogai dreamed of being respectedfor his writing. After facing constant rejection he lost faith in himself and eventually became a demon. His story shows how the desire for acceptance can slowly turn into obsession

    • Appearance

    Kyogai is a tall and muscular demon with pale skin and several large drums attached to his body. His sharp yellow eyes and powerful build give him an intimidating appearance. The drums on his shoulders chest and back are the source of his unique abilites

    Personality

    Kyogai is proud but deeply insecure. He wants others to respect his talent and cannot accept being treated as worthless. Although he becomes violent after turning into a demon he still values his written work and becomes genuinely happy when someone appreciates it

    His final moments reveal that he was never searching for power alone. He simply wanted someone to acknowledge his efforts

    Fighting Style

    Kyogai fights by striking the drums on his body, Every drum creates a different effect allowing him to control the entire mansion during battle. His unpredicatable attacks force opponents to constantly adapt while trying to keep their balance

    Trivia

  • Kyogai was once Lower Rank Six of the Twelve Kizuki

    • Legacy

    Kyogai is remembered as one of the most tragic early demons in Demon Slayer. His desire to be acknowledged made him lose his way but Tanjiro's kindness allowed him to leave the world with the respect he had wanted for so long. His story reminds us that a few sincere words can change someone's final moments forever
