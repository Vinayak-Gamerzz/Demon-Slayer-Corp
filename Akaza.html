About

Akaza is the Upper Rank Three of the Twelve Kizuki and one of the strongest demons in Demon Slayer. He is famous for his incrdible martial arts skills and his desire to fight only the strongest opponents. Unlike many other demons Akaza has no interest in harming people who cannot defend themselves. His only goal is to become stronger and challenge powerful warriors.

Before Becoming a demon Akaza was a human named Hakuji. His life was filled with loss pain heartbreak. After losing the people he cared about he accepted Muzan's blood and left his humanity behind. Even so small pieces ofhis past remained hidden deep within his heart and would later shape his final moments

    He has short pink hair bright yellow eyes and blue markings that cover his face arms and body His appearance is simple yet unforgettable and reflects his fighting spirit. He wears loose white pants with a blue vest allowing him to move freely during battle

    Personality

    Akaza is confident fearless and always searching for stronger opponents. He respect people who dedicate their lives to becoming stronger and often offers skilled fighters the chance to become demons instead of killing them

    Even though he appears ruthless traces of his human ast never completely disapper. The memories of the people he once loved remain buried inside him making his story one of the most tragic among the Upper Rank Demons

    Fighting Style

    Aakaza fights with his bare hands using powerful martial arts instead of weapons. His speed precision and destructive punches allow him to overwhelm even the strongest Demon Slayers. Every movemnt is controlled making him an incredibly dangerous opponent in close combat
