The Insect Hashira

"If we could all just get along, I think that would be wonderful"

About

Shinobu Kocho is the Insect Hashira of the Demon Slayer Corps and one of the most intelligent and compassionate members of the organization. Unlike most Hashira, she lacks the physical strength needed to decapitate demons. Instead, she developed a unique fighting style that uses speed, precision, and deadly wisteria-based poison to defeat her enemies.

Behind her constant smile lies deep grief and a burning desire to avenge her older sister, Kanae Kocho, who was killed by the Upper Rank Two demon, Doma. Despite carrying immense emotional pain, Shinobu continues to fight with kindness, intelligence, and unwavering determination.

    • Appearance

    Shinobu is petite and graceful, with black hair fading into purple at the tips and soft purple eyes.

    She wears a butterfly patterned haori over the standard Demon Slayer Corps uniform. Her Nichirin sword resembles a thin needle, designed for injecting poison instead of cutting off a demon's head.

    Personality

    Shinobu always appears calm, cheerful, and polite, often wearing a gentle smile. However, beneath this peaceful exterior lies intense anger toward demons because of the death of her sister, Kanae.

    She believes demons should be punished, yet she never loses her composure in battle.

    She is highly intelligent, compassionate toward humans, and deeply cares for Kanao and the Butterfly Mansion residents.

    Fighting Style

    Since Shinobu lacks the physical strength to decapitate demons, she created Insect Breathing, a derivative of Flower Breathing. Her style focuses on lightning fast thrusts that inject lethal wisteria poison directly into a demon's body.

    Her speed, agility, and precision make her one of the fastest Hashira.
