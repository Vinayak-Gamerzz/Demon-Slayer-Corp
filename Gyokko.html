About

Gyokko is the Upper Rank Five of the Twelve Kizuki and one of the strongest demons in Demon Slayer. He sees himself as a brilliant artist and belives his terrifying creations are masterpieces. While others are horrified by his work Gyokko takes greate pride in turning pain and death into what he calls art

Before becoming a demon very little was known about his human life. After receiving Muzan's blood he devoteed himself to creating grotesque works that reflected his twisted view of beauty. His arrogance and obsession with perfectionmake him both fascinating and terriying

    • Appearance

    Gyokko has a bizarre Appearance that immediately sets him apart from other demons His mouth is placed where his eyes should be while his actual eyes are scattered across his face and body. He often emerges from decorated porcelain vases and later reveals a stronger form covered in white scales with golden markings

    Personality

    He is proud arrogant and completely obsessed with his own talent. He constantly praises his creations and expects others to admire them even when they are horrifying. Anyone who insults his art quickly becomes the target of his anger

    Although he acts confidently he becomes frustrated whenever someone ignores or criticizes his work showing that his pride is one of his greatest weaknesses

    Fighting Style

    Gyokko fights from a distance using magical porecelain vases that allow him to move instantly across the battlefield. He attacks with strange sea creatures poisonous techniques and powerful transformations that keep his opponents off balance
