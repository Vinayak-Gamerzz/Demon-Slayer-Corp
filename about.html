What is Demon Slayer?

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba is a Japanese manga and anime series created by Koyoharu Gotouge. It follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy whose family is attacked by demons, leaving only his sister Nezuko alive but transformed into a demon. Determined to save her and avenge his family, Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps and begins a journey filled with danger, friendship, and unforgettable battles.
