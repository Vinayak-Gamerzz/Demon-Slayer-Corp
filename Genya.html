About

Genya Shinazugawa is a determined member of the Demon Slayer Corps adn the younger brother of the Wind Hashira, Sanemi Shinazugawa, Unlike MOST dEMON Slayer he is unable to use any breathing styles, Instead of giving up he developes a unique ability that allows him to temporarily gain demonic powers by consuming demon flesh

Although he initially appears cold,rude, and distant, he gradually reveals his compassionate nature and unwavering determination to protect others, His courage, resilience and willingness to sacrifice himself make him one of the most inspiring characters in Demon Slayer
