Muzan Kibutsuji

The Demon King

About

Muzan Kibutsuji is the primary antagonist of Demon Slayer and the progentior of all demons. Over a thousand years ago, he became the first demon after receiving an experimental medicine intended to cure his terminal illness. Although it saved his life, it transformed him into an immortal being with an insatiable hunger for human flesh

As the creator and ruler of all demons, Muzan possesses unmatched strength, intelligence, and regeneration abilities. He constantly seeks the legendary Blue Spider Lily and the secret to conquering sunlight, believing that true perfection can only be achieved by becoming completely invincible

    • Appearance

    Muzan usually appears as a pale man with black curly hair and striking red eyes. He often wears an elegant black suit, white fedora, and matching trousers, giving him the appearance of a wealthy gentleman

    Throughout the series, he frequently changes his appearance, disguising himself as a woman or even a child to avoid detection. During the final battle, his body transforms into a monstrous form covered in mouths, tentacles, and countless whip-like appendages

    Personality

    He is cold, ruthless, arrogant, and completely devoid of empathy. He views every demon as a disposable tool and immediately eliminates anyone who disappoints him

    Obsessed with perfection and survival, Muzan fears only one thing death. His overwhelming pride and desire for absolute immortality drive every decision he makes

    Fighting Style

    Rather than relying on swordsmanship, Muzan fights using his overwhelming physical abilities and mastery over his own body

    His attacks consist of countless whip-like tentacles, bladed appendages, poisonous blood, and rapid shape shifting techniques capable of destroying entire battlefields

    • Legacy
