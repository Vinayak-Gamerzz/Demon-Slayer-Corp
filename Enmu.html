About

Enmu is the Lower Rank One of Twelve Kizuki and the main antagonist of the Mugen Train Arc. Unlike many demons who rely on brute strength, Enmu prefers to manipulate the minds of his victims. He traps people in peaceful dreams before leaving them completely helpless

After Impressing Muzan with his loyalty, Enmu was given more blood and became far stronger than the other Lower Rank Demons. His Intelligence, Patience, and cuel imagination make him one of the most dangerous enemies tanjiro and his friends have faced

    • Appearance

    Enmu has long dark hair with blue highlights and pale skin that gives him a ghostly appearance. His gentle smile and relaxed expression hide his cruel nature. The eyes on his hands display his rank and add to his unsettling appearance

    Personality

    Enmu enjoys watching people suffer. He takes pleasure in giving his victims beautiful dreams before turning them into terrifying nightmares. Calm and soft spoken, he rarely loses his composure and prefers to play with his enemies instead of defeating them quickly

    His greatest desire is to earn Muzan's approval, and he is willing to sacrifice anything to achieve that goal

    Fighting Style

    Enmu focuses on psychological attacks instead of direct combat. He places his enemies into deep sleep, forcing them to face dreams and memories that keep them distracted. During the battle on the Mugen Train, he merges with the train itself, allowing him to attack passengers from every direction

    • Legacy

    Enmu proved that fear is not always created through physical strength. His ability to trap people inside their own dreams made him one of the most unique demons in the series. Even after his defeat, the events of the Mugen Train Arc changed the lives of Tanjiro and his friends forever
