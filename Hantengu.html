Hantengu

Upper Rank Four

"I have done nothing wrong"

About

Hantengu is the Upper Rank Four of the Twelve Kizuki and one of the most unusual demons in Demon Slayer. At first he appears weak frightened and harmless but this is only an illusion. Whenever he is attacked he splits into powerful clones each representing one of his emotions. Together they become far more dangerous than a single opponent

Before becoming a demon Hantengu spent his life blaming others for his actions. He never accepted responsibility and always belived he was the victim. Even after becoming a demon this mindset never changed making him one of the most selfish and deceptive members of the Upper Ranks

    • Appearance

    Hantengu is small thin and elderly in appearance with long gray hair sharp nails and deep wrinkles across his face. His fearful expression makes him look harmless but his true strengthis hidden beneath this fragile appearance.Each of his emotion clones has a completely different look reflecting its unique Personality and power

    Personality

    Hantengu is cowardly dishonest and constantly terrified. He cries and begs for mercy even while attacking others. Rather than admitting his mistakes he always blames someone else for his actions

    His emotions clones reveal different sides of his Personality including anger joy sorrow and pleasure. Together they created an unpredictable enemy who is difficult to understand and even harder to defeat

    Fighting Style

    He avoids direct combat whenever possible. Instead on his emotion clones to overwhelm his enemies from every direction. Each clone has different abilites allowing them to fight together with perfect coordination
