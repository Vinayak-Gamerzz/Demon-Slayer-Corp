About

The Hand Demon is one of the first major demons Tanjiro faces during the Final Selection Arc unlike ordinary Demons, he has survived for decades by devouring countless Demon Slayer candidates. His terrifying appearance and deep hatred for the Demon Slayer Corps make him a memorable early villian

Years ago he was captured by Sankoji Urokodaki and imprisoned on Mount Fujikasane, Unable to escape, he spent years taking revenge by hunting Urokodaki's students during every final selection

    • Appearance

    The Hand Demon has a massive body covered with countless oversized arms growing from every direction. His face is hidden beneath layers of hands which makes him look even more terifying. His enormus size and twisted appearance reflect the many years he spent growing stronger

    Personality

    The Hand Demon is cuel arrogant and driven by revenge. He enjoys hunting young Demon Slayer and takes pride in the fear he creates. His hatred toward Sankonji Urokodaki grows stronger every year because< he blames him for his imprisonment/h4>

    Even after becoming powerful he remains trapped by his anger and never lets go of the past

    Fighting Style

    The Hand Demon relies on overwhelming physical strength. His many arms allow him to attack from every direction while easily grabbing or crushing his opponents. Rather than using complicated techniques he depends on brute force and relentless attacks
