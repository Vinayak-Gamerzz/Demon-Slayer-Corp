Upper Rank Six

About

Gyutaro and Daki share the position pf Upper Rank Six, making them the only pair of demons to hold a single tank together. As, siblings they fight side by side and protect each other without hesitaion. Their bond is the source of both their greatest strength and their greatest weakness

Long Before becoming demons, the two lived extreme poverty and faced constant suffering after a tragic series of events, Muzan's blood gave them a second chance at life, but it also turned them into demons. Evem after losing their humanity, they never abandoned each other, making their story one of the most emotional in the series

    • Appearance

    Gyutaro has a thin body, messy green hair, sharp teeth, and sickly pale skin covered with dark markings. His frightening appearance reflects the harsh life he endured as a human

    Daki has long white hair with green tips and bright green eyes. She is graceful and elegant, often wearing beautiful kimonos that hide her deadly nature

    Personality

    Gyutaro is aggressive, bitter, and fiercely protective of his younger sister. Years of hardship filled him with anger toward the world, but his love for Daki never changed

    Daki is proud, spoiled, and quick to lose her temper. Although she appears confident, she often depends on her brother during difficult battles. Together, they care deeply for each other despite their cruel actions

    Fighting Style

    The siblings fight as a perfect team. Gyutaro attacks with poisoned blood sickles while Daki controls razor sharp obi sashes that strike from every direction. To defeat them both siblings must be beheaded at the same time, making them one of the most difficult Upper Rank demons to overcome.

    Blood Demon Art

    Shared Blood Demon Arts

    Daki controls powerful obi sashes that can slice through buildings and capture her enemies.

    • Legacy
