About

Nezuko Kamado is the younger sister of Tanjiro Kamado and one of the main protagonists of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. After her family is attacked by demons, she is transformed into a demon herself. Despite this, Nezuko retains her human emotions and refuses to harm innocent people

with the help of her brother, she embarks on a journey to regain her humanity while fighting alongside the Demon Slayer corp, her incredible strength, unique blood demon art, and unwavering love for her family make her one of the mose beloved characters in the series
