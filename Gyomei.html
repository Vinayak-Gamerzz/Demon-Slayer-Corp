About

Gyomei Hinmejima is the stone hashira of the demon slater corps and is widely regarded as the strongest hashira of his generation. Despite the immense physical strength, and intimidating Appearance, he is a gentle, compassionate, and deeply spiritual man who constantly prays for those around him

Before joining the Demon slayer corps, gyomei cared for orphaned children at a temple. a tragic demon attack changed his life forever, inspiring him to dedicate himself to protecting others. His unmatched strength, unwavering faith and extraordinary combat skills make him one of the greatest humanity's defenders.

    • Appearance

    Gyomei is the tallest and physically strongest Hashira. He has a muscular build, short black hair, and blank white eyes due to being blind since childhood. He wears the standard demon slayer corps uniform beneath a green haori and carries large prayer beads around his wrists while often shedding tears during prayer

    Instead of a Nichirin sword, he fights using a massive axe connected to a spiked flail by a long chain

    Personality

    Gyomei is calm, humble and deeply religious. He belives in compassion, discipline, and protecting the inncocent. Although he appears intimidating because of his size, he has a gentle heart and mourns every life lost to demons

    His wisdom and kindness earn him respect of every hashira, and hes serves as a mentor and role model within the Corps

    Fighting Style

    Gyomei is the master of stone Breathing, a powerful style that emphasizes overwhelming strength, precision, and destructive froce his unique nichirin axe and flail allow him to attack from both bong and close range with devastating effectiveness

    Even without eyesight his heightened senses enable him to preceive his surroundings with incredible accuracy
