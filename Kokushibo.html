Kokushibo

The Strongest Upper Rank Demon

"No matter how much time passes, true strength is something I can never stop pursuing"

About

Kokushibo is the Upper Rank One of the Twelve Kizuki and the Strongest demon to serve inder Muzan Kibutsuji. Before becoming a demon, he was Michikatsu Tsugikuni, a legendary swordsman and the older twin brother of Yoriichi Tsugikuni, the greatest Demon Slayer in history

Despite being gifted with Extraordinary talent, Michikatsu spent his entire life living in the shadow of his younger brother. His admiration slowly turned into jealousy, and his desperate desire to surpass Yoriichi ultimately led him to accept Muzan's blood and abandon his Humanity

Even after centuries as a demon, Kokushibo never stopped chasing perfection. His calm demeanor, unmatched swordsmanship, and terrifying mastery of Moon Breathing made him one of the most feared beings in existence

    • Appearance

    Kokushibo has one of the most striking appearance in the series. His long black hair, tipped with deep red, flows behind him, while six glowing golden eyes cover his face, giving him an unsettling yet majestic presence

    He wears a dark purple kimono decorated with hexagonal patterns, reflecting his noble origins as a samurai. His Nichirin sword is unlike any other, it appears to be made of living flesh, complete with veins, eyes, and a constantly changing organic appearance

    Every part of his design reflects both elegence and horror, making him instantly recognizable as the strongest of the Upper Ranks

    Personality

    Unlike many demons who enjoy cruelty for its own sake, Kokushibo is calm, disciplined, and composed. He carries himself with the dignity of a warrior and respects those who display genuine strength

    Beneath that calm exterior, however, lies a man consumed by insecurity. His greatest weakness was never a lack of talent it was his inability to accept living in someone else's shadow. His obsession with surpassing Yoriichi slowly overshadowed his love for his family and ultimately cost him everything that once made him human

    Even after centuries, Kokushibo remained trapped by regret, constantly pursuing a perfection that could never truly satisfy him

    Fighting Style

    Kokushibo combines centuries of combat experience with Moon Breathing, a unique Breathing Style that he developed from Sun Breathing before becoming a demon

    His attacks create countless crescent-shaped energy blades that strike from multiple directions, making it nearly impossible for opponents to predict or evade them. Combined with his immense speed, monstrous strength, and regenerative abilities, he becomes one of the deadliest swordsmen in history

    Moon Breathing

    Moon Breathing is one of the most destructive and elegant Breathing Styles in the series.

    Some of its techniques include:

  • First Form : Dark Moon, Evening Palace
  • Third Form : Loathsome Moon, Chains
  • Fifth Form : Moon Spirit Calamitous Eddy
  • Sixth Form : Perpetual Night, Lonely Moon
  • Eighth Form : Moon-Dragon Ringtail
  • Tenth Form : Drilling Slashes, Moon Through Bamboo Leaves
  • Fourteenth Form : Catastrophe, Tenman Crescent Moon
  • Sixteenth Form : Moonbow, Half Moon
  • Final Form: Full Moon

    • Each attack releases razor-sharp crescent blades that relentlessly pursue his enemies

    Relationships

    Yoriichi Tsugikuni

    Yoriichi was Kokushibo's younger twin brother and the greatest swordsman of his era. Although Kokushibo admired his brother's incredible talent, that admiration gradually became jealousy. His inability to surpass Yoriichi shaped the course of his entire life

    Muzan Kibutsuji

    Kokushibo served as Muzan's strongest subordinate for centuries. While he respected Muzan's immense power, his true motivation was always his own pursuit of strength rather than blind loyalty

    Muichiro Tokito

    Muichiro was revealed to be Kokushibo's descendant. Their encounter became one of the most emotional battles in the series, reminding Kokushibo of the family he had abandoned long ago

    Trivia

  • Kokushibo was originally Michikatsu Tsugikuni
  • He is the older twin brother of Yoriichi Tsugikuni
  • He served Muzan for nearly five centuries
  • He is the strongest member of the Twelve Kizuki
  • His six eyes greatly enhance his perception during battle
  • His sword is made from his own flesh
  • He is one of the few demons capable of using a Breathing Style

    • Legacy

    Kokushibo's story is one of the greatest tragedies in Demon Slayer. He was not defeated because he lacked strength he was defeated because he spent his entire life chasing someone else's greatness instead of appreciating his own

    His fall serves as a powerful reminder that envy and obsession can consume even the strongest individuals. Though remembered as the most powerful Upper Rank demon, Kokushibo's story is ultimately one of regret, lost humanity, and the price of abandoning the people who mattered most
