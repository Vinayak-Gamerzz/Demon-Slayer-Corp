Breathing Styles

Master You Breathing and your body will move beyond its limits

Overview

Breathing Styles are powerful swordsmanship techniques used by Demon Slayer Corps to fight demons. By mastering breathing control, Demon Slayers enhance their strength, speed, endurance, and focus, allowing them to battle enemies far stronger than ordinary humans

Each Breathing style follows a unique fighting method and contains multiple Forms designed for diiferent combat Situations

Total Concentration Breathing

Every Breathing style is built upon Total Concentration Breathing, a techinque that increases the amount of oxygen flowing through the body. This greatly improves a swordsman physical abilities and allows them to fight at their full potential

The highest level of mastery is known as Total Concentration Constant, where the breathing techinque is amintained all the time
