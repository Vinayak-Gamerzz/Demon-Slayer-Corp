About

Zenitsu Agatsuma is one of the main protagonists of Demon Slayer and a member of the demon slayer corps, although he is often frightened, anxious, and lacking in confidence, he possesses extraordinary talent as a swordsman

When he falls asleep or loses consciousness during battle, his instincts take over, allowing him to unleash breathing speed and precision with Thunder Breathing his courage, loyalty, and determination make him an invaluable friend and powerful Demon Slayer
