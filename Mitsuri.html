About

Mitsuri Kanroji is the Love Hashira of demon slayer corps and one of its most cheerful and compassionate members. Despite her gentle personality and warm heart, she possesses extraordinary physical strength thanks to her uniquely dense muscles, allowing her to wield a special flexible Nichirin Blade that very few swordsmen could even lift

She joined the demon slayer corps to find someone who would accept and love her for who she truly was. Over time, she became an inspiring warrior whose kindness, optimism, and unwavering courage made her one of the corps most beloved hashira.

    • Appearance

    Mitsuri has long pink hair with lime-green tips, bright green eyes and a friendly smile that reflects her cheerful nature. She wears the standard Demon Slayer corps uniform with a white haori and a distinctive flexible Nichirin Blade, making her appearance unique among the Hashira

    Personality

    Mitsuri is kind, emotional, and incredibly caring toward everyone around her. She values friendship, compassion and love above all else. Although she often blushes easily and expresses her emotions openly, she becomes fearless and determined when innocent lives are in danger.

    Fighting Style

    Mitsuri uses Love Breathing, a style she created herself based on flame breathing. Her swordsmanship relies on flexibility, incredible speed, and unpredictable whip like attacks that overwhelm her opponents from multiple directions.

    Her unusually dense muscles give her immense strength without sacrificing agility
